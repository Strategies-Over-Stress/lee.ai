## THE PROBLEM

### Section Header
"AI Is a Power Tool. Not a Replacement for Judgment."

### Copy
Every week there's a new AI builder promising you can "create an app in minutes." And technically, they're not wrong. You CAN get something working quickly.

But "working" and "ready for your business" are two very different things.

**The math:** Enterprises invested $684 billion in AI in 2025. Over $547 billion — 80%+ — failed to deliver intended business value (RAND Corporation). At your scale, that's not billions. It's three months of evenings wasted on a tool that doesn't work, plus the subscriptions you kept paying "just in case" while you figured it out.

**The security:** 45% of AI-generated code fails basic security tests (Veracode, 2025). 62% contains known vulnerabilities. AI optimizes for "it works," not "it's safe." If you handle customer data, financial records, or anything regulated — that gap between "works" and "safe" can end your business.

**The hidden cost:** AI tools themselves are becoming the new SaaS trap. Monthly tokens. Premium features behind paywalls. Integration fees. You left one subscription treadmill and stepped onto another.

### The Punchline
You don't need to figure this out alone. You don't need to become a developer. You need someone who's been building software for a decade — who uses AI as a tool, not a crutch — and who can tell you what's worth building, what's worth buying, and what's worth ignoring.
